Applying for Financial Aid Instructions

Follow the instructions below to see what financial aid you qualify for.

Click here to return back to SLCC Homepage

1) Complete Student Federal Student Aid ID

A. Go to the FSA ID website B. Create an FSA ID for the Student. Enter student information, including a phone number and email. C. If the student is dependent, create an FSA ID for the parent. HINT: emails and phone numbers must be different for each FSA ID account

2) Apply at Free Application for Federal Student Aid

A. Go to FAFSA B. Enter SLCC Federal School Code: 005220 C. Using information from 2017 taxes, fill out both student and parent tax information. Use IRS Data Retrieval Tool when possible

3) SLCC recieives financial aid information from FASFA website

A. Student should check email he/she provided for their FSA User name. B. Student should check their Bruinmail for updates regarding Financial Aid OR contact the Finanical Aid Office. Student must have a FERPA pin to discuss financial matters over the phone.

4) Student completes additonal forms provided by SLCC

A. Log on to MySLCC B. Click on "Financial Aid and Scholarships" tab C. Navigate to "Financial Aid Requirments" panel D. Complete all forms. Each form should recieve a green checkmark. Checkmarks are given after 3-5 buisiness days

5) Student checks back with SLCC's Financial Aid Office

A. Log on to MySLCC B. Click on "Financial Aid and Scholarships" tab C. Click on "Financial Aid Award" D. Select aid award year. E. Click "accept award"
